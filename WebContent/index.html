LearnIt

A web-based collaborative tool dedicated to helping you master various disciplines.

;

LearnIt is designed to increase your competence through:
♦ Taking tests.
♦ Demonstrating knowledge both to yourself and others.
♦ Execute learned material in novel environments.
♦ Mentor and teach others.
♦ Create an original work that demonstrates competence.

;

Interact with our exciting learning modules while communicating with your peers, mentors, and faculty. Submit your work, link elements to your portfolio, and work your way towards becoming a level 4 learner where you're privileged to edit and expand our content within LearnIt.

;

Visually track the progress of 100-300 competencies per domain using LearnIt's dynamic Gestalt Map. Competencies require the integration of explicit knowledge, tactic knowledge, and experience, which will lead to mastery.
